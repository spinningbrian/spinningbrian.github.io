Writing Music with Synthesizers

Synthesizers allow you to create everything from the wild sound effects that you hear in movies, to bleep blooping experimental music, to fundamentals of fundamental sound waveforms.



Signal Flow in a Synthesizer

Synthesizers typically follow an 'east coast' signal flow that harkens back to Bob Moog's creations of the 1960s.
